Figure is an interactive media element for the learner to complete. The figure shows a router with a switch connected to its Fast Ethernet port, and a computer connected to the switch. The router is connected to another router through a WAN link, and the other router has a switch on its Fast Ethernet port and a computer connected to the switch. Below the network there is a topology table that has the devices and I.P. addresses listed with a few blanks. The learner is provided six I.P. addresses, which need to be placed in the proper spaces in the table.
